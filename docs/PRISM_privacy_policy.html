Privacy Policy — Prism

Effective date: 12 April 2026

Developer: Spectrum Works

Contact: developer@spectrumworks.online

Overview

Prism is a visual clock app designed for neurodivergent children. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of children who use this app. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Short version: Prism collects no personal information. All settings are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us or any third party.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Any information that could identify a specific person

Data stored locally on your device:

Prism stores your configuration preferences (such as time format, colour theme, font size, and event labels) in your device's local storage using the operating system's standard preferences mechanism. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

2. Third-Party Services

Google Fonts

Prism uses the Atkinson Hyperlegible typeface, loaded via the google_fonts Flutter package. On first launch, this package may make a network request to Google's servers to download the font file. After the initial download the font is cached locally and no further network requests are made.

Google's privacy policy governs this request: policies.google.com/privacy. The request contains only standard HTTP metadata (such as your IP address and device type) and does not include any personal information entered by you.

If you are concerned about this request, note that Android and iOS both cache network content aggressively — in practice the request occurs at most once per installation.

No other third-party services

Prism does not use advertising networks, analytics platforms, social media SDKs, or any other third-party service that collects data.

3. Children's Privacy

Prism is designed primarily for use by children aged 4–12. We take children's privacy seriously.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.
  • No account creation is required or possible.
  • No in-app purchases are present.
  • No advertising is shown.
  • The app functions entirely offline after the initial font download described above.

This app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and, to the extent applicable, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR.

4. Home Screen Widget (Android)

On Android, Prism offers an optional home screen widget that displays the current progress bar and time remaining. The widget reads data written by the main app to your device's local storage. No data is transmitted to any server as part of widget operation.

5. Permissions

Prism does not request any device permissions beyond those automatically granted to all apps. It does not access the camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device capability.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data Prism stores exists solely on your device. To delete it:

  • Android: Uninstall the app, or go to Settings → Apps → Prism → Storage → Clear Data.
  • iOS: Uninstall the app. iOS automatically removes all associated local data.

We have no copy of your data and cannot delete it on your behalf because we never receive it.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above and publish the updated policy at the same URL. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
