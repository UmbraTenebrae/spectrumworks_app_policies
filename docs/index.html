Core Features

Everything your arsenal needs.

From the range to the safe — LOADOUT keeps every detail in order.

01
Maintenance Tracking

Interval-based tasks keyed to rounds fired, elapsed time, or both. Color-coded alerts surface what's overdue or coming due across every gun in your collection.

02
Ammo Inventory

Track round counts by caliber, brand, and load. Per-entry low-stock thresholds trigger alerts before you run dry. Counts update automatically when you log range sessions.

03
Range Session Log

Log every session with firearm, rounds fired, and ammo lot used. One tap from the dashboard FAB — round counts and inventory update in a single atomic transaction.

04
Model-Specific Setup

Add a Glock, CZ Shadow 2, AR-15, or Benelli M2 and get a pre-populated task list sourced from manufacturer guidance and community best practices — instantly.

05
Cloud Sync

Sync your full dataset across all devices automatically. Incremental sync means only changed rows are transmitted. Works in the foreground, background, or on demand.

06
Data Export

Export your firearms, maintenance logs, range sessions, and ammo inventory as CSV — filtered by gun or in full. Share to Files, email, or any spreadsheet app.

Maintenance System

Nothing slips through the cracks.

Tasks track intervals in rounds fired, days elapsed, or both simultaneously. The moment a gun is added with a known acquisition date, every time-based task immediately shows the correct status — no manual setup required.

  • Dual-mode intervals
    Set a task to fire after 500 rounds, 30 days, or whichever comes first.
  • Push notifications
    Get notified the day a time-based task comes due — even when the app is closed.
  • Full history log
    Every completed task is recorded with the date and round count at completion.
  • Spare parts tracking
    Scope parts inventory per firearm or mark as universal spares across your collection.
